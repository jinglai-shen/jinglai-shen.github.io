Research and Publications

  • Research Interests

    • Switching and hybrid systems (e.g. complementarity systems and DVIs) and their applications
    • Theory and algorithms of continuous optimization and constrained optimal control with applications to statistics and engineering
    • Dynamics, control and simulation of multibody mechanical and robotic systems
    • Nonlinear control theory and applications
