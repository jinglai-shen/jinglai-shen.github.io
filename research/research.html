Research Interests

  • Complementarity systems and hybrid systems: analysis, control and computation with applications to nonsmooth mechanical robotic, manufacturing and aerospace systems as well as general hybrid systems and constrained dynamic optimization
  • Nonlinear control and computation of multibody mechanical systems: multibody dynamics, motion planning, feedback control, and integration methods with applications to aerospace and robotic systems
  • Nonlinear control theory and applications: controllability analysis, motion planning and stabilization via a variety of nonlinear control techniques
  • Experimental studies of mechanical control systems
